Summary

Interstellar is set in a future where Earth is suffering from catastrophic crop failures. Former NASA pilot Cooper joins a team of scientists who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity. The mission tests the bonds of family, the limits of human endurance, and the fabric of space and time itself.
